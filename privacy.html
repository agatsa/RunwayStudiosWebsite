Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Who We Are

Runway Studios ("we", "us", "our") operates an AI-powered marketing intelligence platform accessible at runwaystudios.co and its subdomains. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and clients.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, and organisation details provided during registration.
  • Ad platform data: Advertising performance metrics retrieved via authorised API connections (Meta Marketing API, Google Ads API, YouTube Data API). This includes impressions, clicks, spend, conversions, keywords, and campaign metadata.
  • Uploaded reports: CSV/Excel files containing advertising performance reports uploaded by users for analysis.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and actions taken within the platform.
  • Communications: Messages sent through our contact form or WhatsApp/Telegram integrations.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Runway Studios platform
  • To generate analytics, reports, and AI-powered recommendations based on your ad data
  • To send performance alerts and summaries via WhatsApp, Telegram, or email (with your consent)
  • To respond to support requests and communications
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Google Ads API Data

When you connect your Google Ads account, we access your advertising data via the Google Ads API using OAuth2 authorisation. This access is read-only — we never create, modify, or delete your campaigns. Data retrieved is used solely to power your analytics dashboard and AI recommendations within our platform. We do not share Google Ads data with any third party, and we do not use it for advertising or resale purposes. You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Google Cloud SQL in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and regular security reviews. OAuth tokens are stored encrypted and refreshed automatically.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at info@runwaystudios.co. Upon request, data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party platforms using their respective APIs:

  • Meta Platforms (Meta Marketing API) — Meta Privacy Policy
  • Google LLC (Google Ads API, YouTube Data API, YouTube Analytics API) — Google Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI API) — used for generating insights and recommendations
  • Google Cloud Platform — infrastructure and database hosting

8. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; correct inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; withdraw consent for data processing; and receive a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@runwaystudios.co.

9. Cookies

Our platform uses session cookies required for authentication and functionality. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. We do not sell data to advertisers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests: info@runwaystudios.co
